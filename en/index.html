Second-hand listings for Bilkent community



Community Rules

  • This service is for Bilkent community only.
  • You need to provide your active Bilkent email to be eligible. Requests that do not meet this criterion will be ignored.
  • All transactions are in cash and on campus.
  • All requests are processed on a first-come, first-served basis. Reservations for more than 72 hours are not allowed.
  • The item will be delivered exclusively to the Bilkent email account owner who has initiated contact with the seller.
  • The users of this service respect the time and effort of others.

How It Works

  1. Browse the available items below.
  2. Click the “Contact Seller” button for the item you want. In your message, let them know if you prefer time-based payment. Schedule a meeting within the next 72 hours to finalize the transaction.
  3. Meet on campus, inspect the item, and finalize the deal face-to-face.

Time-Based Payment Policy

This policy applies to certain items. If you engage in a 30-minute campus conversation with the seller, these eligible items become free or have their price reduced.

  • Items that become free are indicated as Time-Based: FREE
  • Items that receive a discount are indicated as Time-Based: [Discounted Price]
  • Ineligible items are indicated as Time-Based: N/A

This is a one-time offer, meaning each buyer can only benefit from this policy for a single item. The right to benefit from this policy is verified through their university email address. Meetings must be held in public, on-campus locations.
